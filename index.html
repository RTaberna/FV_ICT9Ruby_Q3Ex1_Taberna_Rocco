Mobile Suit Gundam

The ever-growing franchise of the Mobile Suit Gundam or MSG in short has multiple series, characters, Mobile Suits, and Gundams. A lot of these have been produced into model kits and the images shown are the box arts for these. Here are some of my favorites.

ASW-G-08 Gundam Barbatos Lupus

The Gundam Barbatos Lupus comes from the Mobile Suit Gundam: Iron Blooded Orphans series. This Gundam is piloted by the beloved MC, Mikazuki Augus. This iteration of the Gundam Barbatos is an upgrade of the original Gundam Barbatos after Mika's fight against the Ein Grazer. A famous battle that this MS partook in was against the Mobile Armor. After receiving heavy damage, this Gundam was upgraded once more into the Gundam Barbatos Lupus Rex, the King of the Wolves, and the famous Devil of Tekkadan.

X-EX01 Gundam Calibarn

The Gundam Calibarn comes from the Mobile Suit Gundam: Witch From Mercury series. This Gundam is piloted by the MC, Suletta Mercury. This Mobile Suit is built by the small tech company, GUND-ARM. We only see this Gundam in the finale of the series, we mainly see it in its Permet Score 1 but the Permet Score 8 is what most fans prefer. Fighting against the Gundam Aerial Rebuild, this MS slowly hurts Suletta, but ultimately wins over Aerial.

RX-93-ν2 Hi-ν Gundam

The RX-93-v2, a.k.a. the Hi-Nu, is a Gundam from the Mobile Suit Gundam: Char's Counterattack. This Mobile Suit is piloted by the original MC of the whole Gundam franchise, Amuro Ray. This MS is an upgrade of the RX-93, who was ultimately defeated by Char. The Mobile Suit is created by the Earth Federation to fight against the Principality of Zeon.

MD-0031UL Dilanza Sol

The Dilanza Sol is a Mobile Suit that comes from the Mobile Suit Gundam: Witch From Mercury. There isn't much to say about this MS aside from being produced by Jeturk Heavy Machinery. This is one of if not the best grunt Mobile Suit designs in the series, sadly with very little screen time.
